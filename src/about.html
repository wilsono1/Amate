About

Meet Kissy Wilson Mendez a dedicated and highly trained nutritionist with a passion for a holistic approach to well-being. Beyond conventional health, Kissy believes in nurturing mind, body, and spirit. Her commitment extends to family values, recognizing the importance of a supportive environment in the pursuit of a fulfilling life.

Ámate is a haven for nutritious meals and shared moments, emphasizing not just healthy ingredients but also the values that bind families and communities together. With Ámate, the journey to wellness is a transformative experience, promoting a harmonious balance of physical health, emotional resilience, and positive connections.
